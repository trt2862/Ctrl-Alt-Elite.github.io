Such as increasing airport security, by running checks on passports etc, countries are able to see where the person comes from, if their identity is real and to prevent any sort of crime coming into the country. However, in the USA, they mainly screen those who look ‘foreign’ or capable of doing such a crime like terrorism. This is particularly due to the 9/11 attacks and how it affected the people of the country, they are bound to check anyone that looks the part. However, it does not protect anyone as even a white cis male can be a terrorist, and therefore, they are not thorough with the checks.
